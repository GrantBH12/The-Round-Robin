Privacy Policy

Plain-language details about what The Round Robin collects, what stays on your device, and what your options are.

What We Collect

We do not require an account to use this site, and we do not ask for sensitive personal information in our tools.

If you email us, we receive the information you include in that message (like your email address and any details you choose to share) so we can respond.

How We Use Local Storage

Some site features save your preferences and tool progress in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

  • bracketology-board-state: saves your current bracket board assignments.
  • paymon-scores: saves Paymon Score calculation results so they are still there when you return.
  • bracketology-theme: saves your light/dark theme preference.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing site data in your browser, or by using reset/clear options in tools when available.

How Data Is Used

We use saved local settings only to provide core features (for example, restoring your bracket board and theme). We do not sell this information.

We may review high-level traffic or performance information to keep the site working, improve features, and fix issues.

Your Rights and Choices

You can choose not to use tools that store local state. You can also delete browser storage whenever you want.

If you contact us and want your message removed from our records, email us and we will review and process reasonable requests.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at granthansell@gmail.com.

Policy Updates

We may update this page as the site evolves. Changes are effective when posted here.
