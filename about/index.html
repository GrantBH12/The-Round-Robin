About The Round Robin

Why I built this, and what makes it different.

The Idea

I got tired of bracketology that relies on vibes. The NCAA selection committee publishes the exact metrics they use — NET, strength of schedule, quadrant records — and almost nobody actually builds their bracket around them.

The Tool

The bracket builder pulls live data every morning from Warren Nolan and lets you place teams into seed lines using the same resume data the committee sees. No guesswork. Every team's Q1 record, road record, SOS, and more is right there as you build.

What's Next

Women's bracketology is coming next season. After that, the plan is to expand into other sports and events where data-driven analysis can cut through the noise — starting with women's basketball and eventually award shows, drafts, and more.

About Me

I'm Grant. I'm a college basketball fan who got obsessed with how the selection committee actually makes decisions. This site is my attempt to do that analysis in public, with the receipts showing.
